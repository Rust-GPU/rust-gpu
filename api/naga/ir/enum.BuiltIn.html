RayTCurrentMax

ObjectToWorld

WorldToObject

§

HitKind

Read in closest hit and any hit shaders, the type of hit as provided by the intersection function if any, otherwise this is 254 (0xFE) for a front facing triangle and 255 (0xFF) for a back facing triangle
